To get Cursor to open paths in the Inspirato profile, change the "HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\cursor\shell\open\command" from this:
"C:\Users\telar\AppData\Local\Programs\cursor\Cursor.exe" "--open-url" "--" "%1"
to this:
"C:\Users\telar\AppData\Local\Programs\cursor\Cursor.exe" "--user-data-dir=C:\Users\telar\.cursor-inspirato" "--open-url" "--" "%1"
because this:
C:\Users\telar\AppData\Local\Programs\cursor\Cursor.exe --user-data-dir="C:\Users\telar\.cursor-inspirato"
is the way you open that profile in Cursor.
